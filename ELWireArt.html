"EL" (Electroluminescent) wire is wire that lights up when supplied with an electric current. Just plug it in, and you are in business. Each of the EL Wire Art images shown above was photographed while the artwork was resting on a table with a semi-reflective surface. Each of these EL wire art pieces is unique--just one of a kind.
